- Publish on Medium
- Wait 2 weeks
- Then post to LinkedIn (Google won't penalize)

**Option 3: Different Angles**
- Medium: Full technical deep-dive
- LinkedIn: Executive summary + lessons learned

## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### Timing

**Best days to publish**:
- **Medium**: Tuesday, Wednesday (tech audience active)
- **LinkedIn**: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM

**Avoid**:
- Fridays (low engagement)
- Weekends (unless B2C content)
- Holidays

---

### Building Authority

1. **Consistency**: Publish 2-4 articles/month

2. **Series**: Create article series
   ```
   "System Design Series: Part 1 - Load Balancers"
   "System Design Series: Part 2 - Caching Strategies"
   ```

3. **Engage**: Comment on others' articles

4. **Newsletter**: Build email list (Medium) or LinkedIn newsletter

---

### Tracking Success

**Medium Stats to Watch**:
- Views
- Reads (more important than views!)
- Read ratio (reads/views > 40% is good)
- Fans (followers from this article)

**LinkedIn Stats**:
- Impressions
- Engagement rate (likes + comments / impressions)
- Shares
- Profile views spike
